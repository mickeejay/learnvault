# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### What is LearnVault?
LearnVault is a decentralized learning platform built on the Stellar blockchain that rewards scholars for completing courses with LRN tokens. It combines education with blockchain incentives to create a sustainable learning ecosystem.

### How do I get started?
1. Connect your Stellar wallet
2. Browse available courses
3. Enroll in a course
4. Complete lessons and milestones
5. Earn LRN tokens upon completion

### What is LRN?
LRN is the native governance token of LearnVault. You earn LRN by completing courses and can use it for:
- Governance voting
- Creating proposals
- Burning for governance participation incentives
- Trading on supported exchanges

## Courses & Learning

### How do I enroll in a course?
1. Navigate to the Courses section
2. Select a course that interests you
3. Click "Enroll"
4. Start learning!

### Can I get a refund if I don't complete a course?
Refunds are not available once you've enrolled. However, you can pause your progress and resume at any time.

### How long does it take to complete a course?
Course duration varies. Each course displays an estimated completion time. Most courses take 4-12 weeks depending on your pace.

### What happens if I don't complete a course?
Your progress is saved indefinitely. You can resume at any time without penalty.

### How do I download my certificate?
Once you complete a course:
1. Go to "My Courses"
2. Select the completed course
3. Click "Download Certificate"
4. Your PDF certificate will be generated with your name, completion date, and LRN earned

### Can I share my certificate?
Yes! Your certificate includes a verification hash that can be validated on the LearnVault platform. Share the certificate URL or hash with others to prove your completion.

## Tokens & Rewards

### How much LRN do I earn per course?
LRN rewards vary by course difficulty and length:
- Beginner courses: 50-100 LRN
- Intermediate courses: 100-250 LRN
- Advanced courses: 250-500 LRN

### When do I receive my LRN tokens?
Tokens are distributed immediately upon course completion and are sent to your connected Stellar wallet.

### Can I trade my LRN tokens?
Yes, LRN tokens can be traded on supported exchanges. Check the LearnVault website for current trading pairs.

### What is the LRN token burn mechanism?
The LRN burn mechanism allows you to burn tokens to increase your governance participation incentives:
- Burn LRN to vote on proposals
- Burn LRN to create proposals
- Burn LRN to delegate voting power
- Higher burns = higher governance weight

### How do I burn LRN tokens?
1. Go to Governance section
2. Select "Burn LRN"
3. Choose your burn amount and reason
4. Confirm the transaction
5. Your governance incentives will be updated

## Governance & Community

### How does governance work?
LearnVault uses a token-weighted governance model:
1. Holders propose changes or initiatives
2. Community votes on proposals
3. Approved proposals are implemented
4. Voting power is based on LRN holdings and burn history

### How do I create a proposal?
1. Hold at least 1000 LRN tokens
2. Go to Governance > Create Proposal
3. Fill in proposal details
4. Burn LRN tokens to submit
5. Community votes for 7 days

### What is the voting power multiplier?
- Base voting power = LRN balance
- Burn multiplier = 1.5x for each LRN burned
- Delegation bonus = 10-25% depending on burn amount

### Can I delegate my voting power?
Yes! You can delegate your voting power to another address:
1. Go to Governance > Delegation
2. Enter the delegate address
3. Burn LRN to confirm delegation
4. Your voting power is now delegated

## Security & Verification

### What is the anti-sybil verification?
Anti-sybil measures prevent one person from creating multiple accounts to gain unfair advantages:
- Email verification
- Phone verification
- Government ID verification
- Biometric verification

### Why do I need to verify my identity?
Identity verification:
- Prevents fraud and abuse
- Increases your sybil score
- Unlocks higher governance participation
- Protects the community

### What is my sybil score?
Your sybil score (0-100) indicates how verified you are:
- 0-50: High risk (limited governance)
- 50-75: Medium risk (standard governance)
- 75-100: Low risk (full governance access)

### How do I improve my sybil score?
Complete verification methods:
1. Verify your email
2. Verify your phone number
3. Verify government ID
4. Complete biometric verification

### Is my personal information safe?
Yes. All personal information is:
- Encrypted end-to-end
- Stored securely
- Never shared with third parties
- Compliant with GDPR and privacy laws

## Troubleshooting

### My wallet won't connect
- Ensure your wallet extension is installed
- Try refreshing the page
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Try a different browser
- Check wallet is on Stellar public network

### I didn't receive my LRN tokens
- Wait 5-10 minutes for blockchain confirmation
- Check your Stellar wallet address
- Verify course completion status
- Contact support if issue persists

### My certificate won't download
- Ensure you've completed the course
- Try a different browser
- Disable ad blockers
- Check your internet connection
- Contact support

### I can't vote on proposals
- Verify you hold LRN tokens
- Complete identity verification
- Check your sybil score
- Ensure proposal voting period is active
